Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • Cashews
  • Paneer
  • Onions
  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Cream

Method of Preparation:

In a blender, grind soaked cashews into a smooth paste. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute. Then, add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt. Cook the masala until it releases oil. Add the cashew paste and cook for a couple of minutes. Add cubed paneer and mix gently, ensuring the paneer is coated with the masala. Pour in cream and stir well. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the curry thickens. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan or rice. Enjoy your homemade Paneer Butter Masala!

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • ginger garlic paste
  • salt to taste
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • bay leaves
  • 4-5 cloves
  • onion
  • green chillies
  • tomatoes
  • coriender and mint leaves

Method of Preparation:

Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1-2 hours. Cook the soaked basmati rice with bay leaves, cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon, and salt until 70-80% cooked. In a separate pan, fry thinly sliced onions until golden brown. Set aside some for garnishing. In the same pan, add oil or ghee, cumin seeds, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until fragrant. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until the masala thickens. Add marinated chicken and cook until it's partially cooked.In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer half of the partially cooked rice, followed by the chicken masala, and then the remaining rice. Top with fried onions, fresh coriander, mint leaves, and saffron milk. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend. Gently mix the layers before serving. Enjoy your delicious Chicken Biryani!
